The Peak of Music

Throughout the years music has had its peaks but we will be talking about the 80s.

80s music was something different. it was well known for soul pop and poprock, aswell as its artists. Artists including Michael Jackson, Madonna, And Prince. Not everything was soul pop and poprock, but it still had Disco, as well as Disco Funk.
